About

LETHALBOTS

As future engineers we see the lack of enthusiasm to be with technology and interest about technology among undergraduate students of Sri Lanka. As an attempt to address this matter we, undergraduates of SLTC have decided to do a Tournament which involves our technological knowledge. SLTC LETHALBOTS will be an epic ROBOTICS Battle between remote control robots which are made using the knowledge shared within students and inter universities.
